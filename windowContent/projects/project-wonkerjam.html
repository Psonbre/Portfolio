Wonkerjam

October 2025

Description

Wonkerjam is the winner of the Fall WonderJam 2025. With the theme being "Counterfeit", Wonkerjam is a fake version of the Wonderjam itself. In reality the game is a compilation of minigames, all parodying or referencing games made by other teams during previous Wonderjams.
