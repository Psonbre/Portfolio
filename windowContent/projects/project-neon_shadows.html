Neon Shadows

August 2021

Description

Neon Shadows is a game developed in 48 hours for the Mini Jam 86, with the constraint "no stopping." In this stealth game, the player is constantly pursued by their shadow, which follows them with a slight delay. This mechanic prevents the player from stopping, adding a layer of tension and challenge to the gameplay experience.
