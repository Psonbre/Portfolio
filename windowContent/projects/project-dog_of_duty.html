Dog Of Duty

December 2022

Description

Dog Of Duty is a game developed in 48 hours during the 2022 Valleyfield Intercollegiate Game Jam. The game is a tower defense where an army of dogs must scare off several waves of dangerous mailmen.

Additional Information

  • 2nd place in the game jam and winner of the $750 prize.
