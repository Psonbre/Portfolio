Goblin's Greed

July 2023

Description

Goblin's Greed is a mix between a top-down hack and slash and a tower defense game. The goal is to grow your treasure hoard while protecting it from heroes trying to steal it. You can fill your dungeon with traps or improve your combat abilities to take them on in a fair fight.

Additional Information

  • Solo-developed
  • Created in 48 hours for the GMTK Game Jam 2023 with the theme "Roles Reversed"
  • Randomly generated dungeon
