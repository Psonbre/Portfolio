Chukkult

January 2024

Description

Chukkult is a point-and-click infiltration game. The goal is to infiltrate a cult by analyzing the behavior of the people who enter before you. Their clothes, their way of speaking, and the secret codes change every day, so you need to stay alert!

Additional Information

  • Developed in 48 hours for the 2024 Valleyfield Intercollegiate Game Jam
  • Theme: Cult
