Collateral Damage

February - April 2024

Description

Collateral Damage is an 8-player platform-fighter with a fully destructible physical environment. Every pixel can be destroyed by the various weapons and abilities available, and the terrain changes accordingly.

Additional Information

  • Developed as a team
  • Responsible for destruction physics, multiplayer, and visuals
  • Project completed as part of a school project over 11 weeks
